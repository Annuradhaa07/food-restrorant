MR. GOURAV KAMBOJ AND
mR. dEEPANSHU KAMBOJ

FOUNDER & DIRECTOR CHAICHURIINDIA PVT. LTD

The start of Chai Churi was both accidental and planned. While traveling, Gourav and Deepanshu discovered a dhaba serving “desi ghee churi.” This inspired them to merge churi with chai. The idea was deeply connected to family traditions and nostalgic memories of desi ghee, warm rotis, and tasty churi.

In a bold move, Chai Churi offered free churi for two months to introduce their unique product. This not only demonstrated their belief in its quality but also their dedication to customer happiness. By April, the tri-city area was buzzing with the flavor of Chai Churi’s chai and churi.

Our Roots

  • In the past, Churi was more than just a snack; it symbolized our hospitality and love, shared with guests as a welcoming gesture.
  • The art of making Churi has been passed down through generations, from mothers to daughters and grandmothers to grandchildren, preserving this cherished tradition.
  • Churi is not just a crispy delight but a link to our heritage, evoking cherished memories and connecting us to our roots.
  • At Chai Churi , each flavour of our Churi is infused with traditional tastes, continuing the long-standing tradition of sharing.

Our Inspiration

  • We support ancient customs, creating a relationship with the past while accepting the present.
  • Reconnect today’s generation with their roots & ensuring that timeless traditions remain alive and celebrated.
  • We strive to create a space where people from all walks of life can gather, share stories, & create lasting memories over delicious churi and fragrant chai.
